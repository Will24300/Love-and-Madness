In a few words

Our kitchen

French cuisine meets herbs and spices from here and elsewhere.

L'Amour et la Folie offers "familiar" dishes and desires for original or different flavors.

Throughout the four seasons, L'Amour et la Folie reinvents its menu to offer a cuisine that opens up to the best products, most often from short or organic circuits!

A place of honor and choice is given to rare and seasonal herbs and vegetables ! Watercress, candied onions, carrots, vitelotte (one of the tastiest purple potatoes), parsnips pair up with ginger, cardamom, tonka or rosemary...

A happy and surprising cuisine made of love and madness!

Our wines

The wine list should be read as a collection of favourites . The wines of friends to share with friends. With the moderation that love knows how to impose on madness!

Made of beautiful encounters and of high standards , l'Amour et la Folie offers a selection of wines to discover at a reasonable price. Mercurey, Pommard, Saumur-Champigny (L'Amour et la Folie is very fond of Burgundy and Loire wines!), Châteauneuf du Pape... Leaving the beaten track, passing through a few paths made of sweet follies, the wines offered here have been chosen on the basis of a single criterion: love!
